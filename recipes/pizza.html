How to make Pizza

You need the following ingredients for the dough:

  • 500g flour
  • 1 cube of yeast
  • 250ml water
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 pinch of sugar

Toppings:

These are requried but use any (NO PINEAPPLE!) toppings you like...

  • 1 container of tomato sauce
  • 3 spoons of olive oil
  • As much cheese as you want

Follow these steps to create the perfect Pizza:

  1. Mix water, yeast, salt, oil and the sugar. Afterwards add in the flour.
  2. Knead the dough until it is smooth.
  3. Wait 30 minutes. The size of the dough should double.
  4. Roll the dough and put toppings on it.
  5. Put it in the oven at 180 Degrees Celsius.
