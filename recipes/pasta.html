How to make Pasta

You need the following ingredients for the Pasta:

  • 500g spaghetti
  • 500g of pre-cut tomatos
  • 2 table-spoons of butter
  • 1 table-spoon of flour
  • 1 cup of cream
  • Salt and Pepper
  • A handfull of Basil
  • Parmesan

Follow these steps to create the perfect Pasta:

  1. Cook the pasta in salt-water.
  2. At the same time melt the butter in a pot and add the flour.
  3. Add the cream and in the end also add the tomatoes.
  4. Cook for a maximum of 10 minutes.
  5. Serve with parmesan and basil.
